About

I'm Jeriko Kammerer — a developer whose work spans the full stack of computing, from writing Python for AI and scientific applications to designing hardware at the register-transfer level in Verilog.

My experience with the Small Satellite Research Lab involved building Helmholtz cage control software for testing satellite attitude determination systems — bridging physics, embedded control, and clean software design.

Outside of lab work, I explore AI/ML, build computational math tools, and design digital circuits including VGA display drivers and memory controllers. I'm driven by the belief that the best engineers understand the full stack — from transistors to neural networks.
